Daily Macronutrient Targets:

  • Protein: 1.6-2.2g per kg of body weight

  • Vegetables: Dark leafy greens, broccoli, peppers, carrots
  • Fruits: Berries, bananas, apples (in moderation)

Exercise Instructions & Tips

Form First, Weight Second

Always prioritize proper form over lifting heavier weights. Start with lighter weights to master the movement pattern, then gradually increase the load.

Progressive Overload

Aim to increase weight, reps, or sets gradually each week. Even small improvements compound over time to create significant results.

Rest Periods

Strength training: 2-3 minutes between sets. Hypertrophy: 60-90 seconds. HIIT/Circuit: 30-60 seconds or as specified.

Warm-Up & Cool-Down

Always spend 5-10 minutes warming up before training and 5-10 minutes cooling down after. This reduces injury risk and improves recovery.

Listen to Your Body

Some muscle soreness is normal, but sharp pain is not. If something doesn't feel right, stop the exercise and consult with a professional.
